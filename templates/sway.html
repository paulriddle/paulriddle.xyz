Additional stuff

I remind you to define a shell alias for editing your Sway config. For example I have 

      visway  -> vi ~/.config/sway/config
      viflash -> vi ~/.config/flashfocus/flashfocus.yml
Using fish? Define abbreviations instead of aliases for this kind of stuff: 
      abbr --add viflash 'vi ~/.config/flashfocus/flashfocus.yml'
View all your defined abbreviations with abbr -s or in 
      ~/.config/fish/fish_variables
